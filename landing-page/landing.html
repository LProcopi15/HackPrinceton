About Designated

DD'ing for your friends? We're here to reward you! We believe that designated drivers should be the VIPs for the night, every night. With Designated you'll recieve coupons and earn money, every time you drive your drunk friends home.

About Us

Based out of The University of Virginia, we are a team of student developers passionate about building impactful web and mobile applications. We built Designated - an app that rewards individuals for using a designated driver - at the Princeton Hackathon in the fall of 2015.
